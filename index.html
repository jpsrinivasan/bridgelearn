🛡️5 Rules Every Child Must Know
🧒
Rule 1
My Body Belongs to ME
Your body is yours alone. Nobody has the right to touch you in a way that feels wrong — not even family. Tap to read the full story.
🔐
Rule 2
Safe vs Unsafe Secrets
Safe secrets are happy surprises. Unsafe secrets make you scared. ALWAYS tell a trusted adult about unsafe secrets.
💻
Rule 3
Online Safety Rules
Online strangers are real strangers. Never share photos, personal info or meet anyone you only know online.
🗣️
Rule 4
Use Your Voice — Always Tell
If something feels wrong — TELL a trusted adult. Keep telling until someone helps. It is NEVER your fault.
🔑
Rule 5
The Family Code Word
Create a secret word with your family. If anyone picks you up without knowing the word — don’t go. Code words save lives!
📖Safety Stories
🚦Traffic Safety
🚦
Traffic Signals
🔴 Red = STOP always · 🟡 Yellow = Prepare to stop · 🟢 Green = Go (check first!). Never cross on red — even if no car visible.
🚶
Crossing the Road
🇮🇳🇺🇸 Look RIGHT → LEFT → RIGHT. 🇳🇿🦘 Look LEFT → RIGHT → LEFT. Always use zebra crossing. Make eye contact with driver first.
🚗
Car Safety
Always wear seatbelt — even in back seat. Never distract driver. Under 12 must use child seat. NEVER get in car with drunk driver.
🚲
Cycling Safety
Always wear helmet — it saves lives. Hand signals for turning. Never use phone while riding. Lights at night — white front, red rear.
👨‍👩‍👧Parent Guide
💬 How to Talk to Your Child About Safety
✅ DO say:
"Your body belongs to you — always."
"You can tell me anything. I will always believe you."
"It is NEVER your fault if something bad happens."
"You will never get in trouble for telling the truth."
❌ NEVER say:
"Don’t embarrass the family."
"Are you sure that really happened?"
"They didn’t mean it — they’re family."
🔑 Family Code Word Activity:
Choose a word your family knows — like MANGO 🥭 or SUNSHINE ☀️
Anyone picking up your child must know this word. If they don’t — child must NOT go and must tell teacher immediately.
Practice this once a month. Make it fun!
