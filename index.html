🛡️ Safety First

🚫 Stranger Danger

Never talk to strangers online. Never share your name, address, school, or photos.

🔐 Keep Passwords Secret

Never share passwords with anyone — even friends. A strong password has letters, numbers and symbols.

📵 Screen Time Rules

Take breaks every 30 minutes. No screens 1 hour before bedtime. Ask parents before downloading apps.

🆘 Tell a Trusted Adult

If anyone online makes you feel scared or uncomfortable, tell a parent or teacher immediately.

🚗 Road Safety

Always look both ways before crossing. Wear seatbelts. Wear helmets when cycling.

💊 Medicine Safety

Never take medicine without a parent. Some pills look like candy — never eat unknown pills.

🤝 Body Safety

Your body belongs to you. No one has the right to touch you in a way that makes you uncomfortable.

🔥 Fire Safety

Never play with matches. Know your home escape plan. Call 911 in emergencies.
